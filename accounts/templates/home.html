Farmix Smart

Agriculture is the principal source of livelihood for more than 40 percent of the population of this state. According to Food and Agricultural Organization (FAO) researchers, between 2010 and 2050 the world population will increase by one third. The demand for crop production will increase by 60 percent higher than the current production. Hence prediction plays a major role to find out the demand of crop production for maximizing the yield. Machine learning is one such technique employed to predict the crop yield using Decision tree regressor algorithm the crops data are collected here with the appropriate parameter like crop variety, Average temperature, Average rainfall, and pesticide tons per hectare which helps the crops to achieve a successful growth. The users are encouraged to enter parameter like country, crop variety, Average rainfall, Average temperature and pesticides per tones of hectare in order to start the prediction process. Diseases in plants/crops are the serious causes in degrading the production quantity and quality, which results in economy losses. Thus, identification of the diseases in plants is very important. Plant disease symptoms are evident in various parts of plants. However, plant leaves are most commonly used to detect the infection. ANN techniques are utilized by several researchers to automate the detection of plant diseases using leaf images. So that, a plant diseases detection system using Artificial Neural Network technique was created and it detect the disease affected plants. In addition, a question-and-answer platform was created especially for farmers so, the user will make their question as the public post and the verified user or the public user will give their opinion for that problem.
