Farmix Smart

Agriculture is the principal source of livelihood for more than 40 percent of the population of this state. According to Food and Agricultural Organization (FAO) researchers, between 2010 and 2050 the world population will increase by one third. The demand for crop production will increase by 60percent higher than the current production.

Hence prediction plays a major role to find out the demand of crop production for maximizing the yield. For that in this paper we propose a prediction method for the major crops using Decision tree regressor. In this project the seed data crops are collected here ,with the appropriate parameter like crop variety, Average temperature , Average rainfall, and pesticide tones per hectare which helps the crops to achieve a successful growth.

In addition we detect the plant diseases detection. Diseases in plants/crops are the serious causes in degrading the production quantity and quality, which results in economy losses. Thus, identification of the diseases in plants is very important. Plant disease symptoms are evident in various parts of plants. However, plant leaves are most commonly used to detect the infection. ANN techniques are utilized by several researchers to automate the detection of plant diseases using leaf images. Also we create a question and answer platform for formers .In addition the web page is being created. The users are encouraged to enter parameter like country, crop variety, Average rainfall, Average temperature and pesticides per tones of hectare in order to start the prediction process.
